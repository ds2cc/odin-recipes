Pizza Recipe

This is a keto-friendly pizza recipe using a fathead crust.

Enjoy a delicious pizza without the carbs!


Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 2 tablespoons warm water
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup almond flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Combine ingredients
  3. Form the dough
  4. Roll out the dough
  5. Add toppings
  6. Bake the pizza
  7. Enjoy your pizza
